About Us

Explore Botanic Haven as a contributor; our interactive website allows you to explore our vast array of plants, manage donations, and keep track of our ever-growing inventory. With features like viewing our newly designed landing page, managing plant collections, and even donating plants with ease, we ensure an engaging and seamless experience.

At Botanic Haven, we're more than just a botanical shop; we're a vibrant community dedicated to nurturing a greener, more vibrant world. Our platform is not only a showcase of stunning plant varieties but also a hub for botanical enthusiasts to contribute and manage our expanding collection. Whether you're donating a plant or managing our inventory, your engagement helps us cultivate a magnificent botanical paradise.

Join us at Botanic Haven, where nature's splendor fills your life with color and serenity. Together, let's create a blooming oasis that celebrates the beauty and diversity of the plant kingdom.

Plants in Our Haven



Welcome to Botanic Haven, your premier destination for exploring a kaleidoscope of botanical wonders. Nestled in our verdant oasis, we pride ourselves on fostering a community where both novice gardeners and experienced horticulturists can thrive. Our diverse collection ranges from the vibrant Rosy Pussytoes to the unique Globe Cactus, each species offering its own distinct beauty and charm.


  1. Rosy Pussytoes: These maroon-hued beauties add a touch of elegance to any garden.
  2. Parrot-lily: Vibrant turquoise blooms that evoke the lushness of the tropics.
  3. Hudson Bay Currant: Sporting striking fuchsia shades, this plant is both captivating and elegant.
  4. Dentate False Pennyroyal: Fiery red leaves that bring a burst of color to your landscape.
  5. Dot Lichen: With deep crimson hues, these lichens are nature's artwork.
  6. Southern Swamp Aster: Delicate pink petals that brighten up any outdoor space.
  7. Forest Naupaka: The subtle khaki tones of this plant blend seamlessly with natural surroundings.
  8. Madrone: Blue-green foliage and intricate branches make this a standout in any garden.
  9. Globe Cactus: Bold and striking in various shades, from black and gray to vibrant orange.
  10. Snake's Head Fritillary: With its stunning magenta petals, brings a burst of color and life to any garden.
